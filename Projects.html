CRS Predictor - Canadian Permanent Residency Helper

As a person who went through tedious and difficult PR application process, I wanted to help people who are going through same experience. This application focuses on personalization of their Canadian Permanent Residency grant experience, and it is the full stack MLOps project from backend to frontend with AI.

Flask, Jquery AWS EC2 Ubuntu, AWS RDS, AWS Route 53 MySQL, Apache Spark REST-API Time series forecasting Regression Model Javascript/HTML/CSS
